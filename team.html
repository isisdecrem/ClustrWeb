Why Clustr?

As an officer in 3 clubs, I found it extremely difficult to keep members on the same page. Not only would each of those clubs use different platforms for management, but would also have multiple means of communication within to cater to all members as some would lack a Facebook account, while others would only use services such as Discord. Seeing this, I joined Clustr to help build a better interface and managerial tool for club officers, but also to cater to members by means of making more accessible the ability to find clubs to join and to receive updates and event details in a friendly manner.

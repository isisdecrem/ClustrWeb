How it works

Clustr+ is an app that is used to connect clubs. We bridge the gap between students and their school's clubs because we believe this is a fundamental for every school. Here are our main features:

All your clubs in one place

Say goodbye to the days of relying on club day or looking through a PDF- find all of your schools clubs on Clustr+.

Admin Access

Club leaders and school staff will have the ability to control their clubs pages. They'll be able to edit club information, post updates, and post events to keep the school up to date.

Schoolwide Availibility

Every student will be able to access their school's clubs by simply inputting their school code into the Clustr+ app. Students do not need to make accounts and will be able to download this app free of charge.

Our Mission

We believe that through connecting students to their clubs, engagement within clubs will skyrocket. We value student's connecting to their school environment and engaging in clubs. Through engagement in clubs, students will be able to discover what they are passionate about, make friends, and feel like a larger part of their community. Every student deserves to be able to find a community within their school that they love. School clubs have the ability to do so much for students and limited accessibility to clubs should not be what prevents students from joining. The current system schools use to familiarize students and clubs is broken: many schools rely on just one club fair a year to get students to sign up for clubs. Schools who do offer students comprehensive lists of clubs often do so with a PDF document hidden on their website. The clubs at schools also have no standard way of communicating with their members. Some clubs use Instagram, others use email lists, some have Facebook groups. Students need ONE place to browse their schools clubs. Clustr is that place.

Lets give students a tool to help their high school careers.
